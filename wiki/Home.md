# VibeBar: Bridge the Gap Between You and Your AI

VibeBar is a floating toolbar that lives above your workflow and helps you communicate better with AI coding agents. 

Think of it this way: you're working in Cursor, Claude Code, or any other AI development environment. You spot something off in the code. You know what's wrong. But explaining it to your AI in a way that actually gets the right fix? That takes time. You're context-switching from coding to prompting. You're trying to gather the right pieces of information. You're rephrasing. You're starting over.

VibeBar eliminates that friction.

## What You Get

- **Project-aware prompts**: Context from your actual codebase, not generic examples
- **One-click audits**: Security, performance, error handling, logging reviewed instantly
- **Smart snipping**: Capture exactly what your AI needs to understand the problem
- **Terminal integration**: Debug faster by bringing your workflow together
- **Real understanding**: VibeBar actually knows your project structure, your tech stack, your patterns

## Why This Exists

AI is incredible. It's also frustrating sometimes. The frustration isn't usually because the AI isn't smart enough. It's because the communication gap is real. You know what you want to fix. Your AI doesn't. That gap wastes time.

We built VibeBar to close that gap.

## The Philosophy

The future of development isn't AI replacing you. It's you and AI working together efficiently. The bottleneck isn't computation or intelligence anymore. It's communication. 

Every feature in VibeBar exists to reduce friction in that communication. To make prompting as natural as coding. To make context flowing as automatic as your IDE's autocomplete.

## Who This Is For

- Developers using AI coding agents regularly
- Teams wanting to standardize AI workflows
- Anyone frustrated with the "craft the perfect prompt" dance
- Projects where consistency and quality matter
- Developers who want to learn better prompting habits

## Why Open Source

VibeBar works best when it's shaped by the people using it. Your project's patterns are different from ours. Your security concerns are specific. Your workflow is unique. A closed tool can't adapt to that diversity.

By being open source, VibeBar becomes something the community builds together. Better prompts. Better audits. Better integrations. Better solutions to problems we haven't even discovered yet.
